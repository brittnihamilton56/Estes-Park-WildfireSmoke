Estes Park School District Wildfire Smoke Planning Tool

A GitHub-ready decision tool for comparing two engineering alternatives for wildfire smoke protection across the district's four school buildings. It estimates order-of-magnitude cost, scores each option with MCDA, and makes one final recommendation while still showing both alternatives.
